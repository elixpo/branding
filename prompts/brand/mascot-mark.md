# Elixpo mascot mark - line-art (logo, no text)

The standalone Oreo logo mark in the **line-art** register (see
[`references/OREO-LINEART.md`](../../references/OREO-LINEART.md)) - the panda
alone, drawn as one continuous line, no wordmark. Used as the GitHub avatar,
favicon, app icon and loading mark. Reads cleanly when shrunk to 32×32.

## Prompt
A clean, centered logo mark of the Oreo panda drawn as a single continuous
one-line drawing - one unbroken, very thin, single-weight ink line
rgb(33,33,33) that never lifts the pen, with a few gentle loops, loose and
minimal. A cute, happy, healthy chibi panda in a relaxed compact sitting pose,
shown as a centered emblem with generous even margin on all sides so it reads
at small sizes down to 32×32, perfectly balanced. The face is SUGGESTED with a
few simple strokes - cute gleamy sparkling eyes with tiny white catch-lights and
a small friendly smile - never a detailed, shaded or filled face. Both ears
filled coral rgb(255,119,89), and one leg filled coral rgb(255,119,89); a small,
compact, perfectly round red rgb(220,60,50) chest badge centered on the chest
carrying a single bold clean capital letter "E" (the only letter in the whole
mark). Everything else is the one thin ink line. Flat 2D vector line art, NO
solid fills anywhere except the two coral ears, the one coral leg and the red
"E" badge; no 3D, no gradients, no glow, no shadows, no detailed rendering, no
pixel art, no thick outline. Plain flat white background rgb(255,255,255), the
emblem centered with generous even margin, no extra text, no wordmark, no
watermark.
