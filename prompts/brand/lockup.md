# Elixpo primary lockup (mascot mark + wordmark)

The primary horizontal logo: the Oreo mark to the left, the "Elixpo"
wordmark to the right. This is the default Elixpo logo — site header,
README hero, slide decks.

## Prompt
A horizontal brand logo lockup: on the LEFT, the Oreo panda mascot crest
as a compact emblem; on the RIGHT, the wordmark spelling the single word
"Elixpo" in chunky rounded friendly pixel-art letters, correctly spelled
E-l-i-x-p-o, lowercase with a capital E, on one baseline, vertically
centered against the mascot. Featuring the Oreo panda mascot: warm
cream-white fur rgb(240,238,232), dark rounded ear and eye patches
rgb(38,38,48), big sparkly black eyes with white catchlights, rosy pink
cheeks rgb(255,93,104), and a round red chest badge rgb(220,60,50) bearing
a single bold clean capital letter E in cream-white and nothing else. The
wordmark letters are warm coral-red rgb(255,93,104) with a thick dark
outline rgb(38,38,48) and a soft warm-gold rgb(255,190,30) accent. Balanced
spacing between mark and wordmark, pixel art cartoon style, thick uniform
dark outline, vibrant warm celebration colours, cute kawaii style, flat
even lighting, warm cream white background rgb(255,248,235), wide
horizontal crop, no tagline, no extra words, no watermark.
