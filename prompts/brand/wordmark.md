# Elixpo wordmark (text logotype + tagline, no mascot)

The "Elixpo" logotype on its own — no panda — with the **"Built in the
Open"** tagline beneath. Used inline in footers, letterhead, and anywhere
the mark would be redundant. Text colours are drawn from the panda palette
(charcoal patches + the red E-badge) so the wordmark reads as the same
brand as the mascot.

## Prompt
A bold two-line brand wordmark on a warm cream background. TOP LINE: the
single word "Elixpo" in chunky rounded friendly pixel-art letters,
correctly and clearly spelled E-l-i-x-p-o, one word, lowercase except a
capital E, letters evenly kerned on one horizontal baseline. SECOND LINE,
directly beneath and centered, in much smaller clean rounded letters: the
tagline "Built in the Open" — three words, correctly spelled, Title Case.
The "Elixpo" letters are deep charcoal rgb(38,38,48) matching the panda's
dark patches, with a thick uniform dark outline; the capital E is filled
in the signature badge-red rgb(220,60,50) to echo the mascot's chest
E-badge, with a soft warm-gold rgb(255,190,30) accent glow. The tagline is
in warm muted brown-pink rgb(160,120,100). Both lines centered on a shared
vertical axis with comfortable spacing. Crisp legible typography, cute
kawaii pixel-art brand feel, no mascot, no panda, no other words. Flat even
lighting, warm cream white background rgb(255,248,235), wide horizontal
16:9 crop, no watermark.
