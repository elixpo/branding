# Elixpo wordmark - line-art (text logotype + tagline, no mascot)

The "Elixpo" logotype on its own - no panda - in the **line-art / editorial**
register (see [`references/OREO-LINEART.md`](../../references/OREO-LINEART.md)),
with a small **"Built in the Open"** tagline beneath. Used inline in footers,
letterhead, and anywhere the mascot would be redundant. Colours come from the
line-art palette (ink + the red E-badge + a coral underline) so the wordmark
reads as the same brand as the mascot.

## Prompt
A clean, minimal editorial brand wordmark on a plain flat white background
rgb(255,255,255), in a calm tech-minimalism style with generous airy margins.
TOP LINE (the hero): the single word "Elixpo" - correctly and clearly spelled
E-l-i-x-p-o, one word, a capital E then lowercase l-i-x-p-o, evenly kerned on
one horizontal baseline - set as an elegant high-contrast serif logotype
(Fraunces / Playfair feel) in ink rgb(33,33,33). The capital "E" is tinted the
signature badge-red rgb(220,60,50) to echo the mascot's chest badge. Directly
beneath the word, a single heavy coral rgb(255,119,89) underline bar, flat and
clean. SECOND LINE, centered below the underline in a MUCH SMALLER uppercase,
letter-tracked monospace (Space Mono feel), muted slate rgb(147,147,159): the
tagline "Built in the Open", three words, correctly spelled. Flat 2D vector,
fine single-weight strokes, crisp legible typography. NO 3D, no gradients, no
glow, no shadows, no pixel art, no mascot, no panda, no doodles, no other words.
Plain white background, wide horizontal 16:9 crop, no watermark.
