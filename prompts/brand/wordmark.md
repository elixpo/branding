# Elixpo wordmark (text logotype, no mascot)

The "Elixpo" logotype on its own — no panda. Used inline in footers,
letterhead, and anywhere the mark would be redundant.

## Prompt
A bold wordmark logotype spelling the single word "Elixpo" in chunky
rounded friendly pixel-art letters, correctly and clearly spelled
E-l-i-x-p-o, one word, lowercase except a capital E, letters evenly
kerned on a single horizontal baseline and horizontally centered. The
letters are warm coral-red rgb(255,93,104) with a thick uniform dark
outline rgb(38,38,48) and a soft warm-gold rgb(255,190,30) drop accent,
playful celebratory cartoon style matching a cute kawaii pixel-art brand.
Crisp legible typography, no mascot, no panda, no extra words, no tagline.
Flat even lighting, warm cream white background rgb(255,248,235), wide
horizontal crop, no watermark.
