# Elixpo wordmark (text logotype + tagline + doodles, no mascot)

The "Elixpo" logotype on its own — no panda — with a small **"Built in the
Open"** tagline beneath and light on-brand doodles scattered around it.
Used inline in footers, letterhead, and anywhere the mark would be
redundant. Text colours are drawn from the panda palette (charcoal patches
+ the red E-badge) so the wordmark reads as the same brand as the mascot.

## Prompt
A polished, well-balanced two-line brand wordmark on a warm cream
background. TOP LINE (large, the hero): the single word "Elixpo" in chunky
rounded friendly pixel-art letters, correctly and clearly spelled
E-l-i-x-p-o, one word, lowercase except a capital E, evenly kerned on one
horizontal baseline, with a subtle soft drop shadow giving it a clean
sticker-like lift. SECOND LINE, directly beneath and centered, in a MUCH
SMALLER delicate rounded font — roughly a third the height of the
wordmark — the tagline "Built in the Open", three words, correctly
spelled, Title Case, with relaxed letter-spacing. The "Elixpo" letters are
deep charcoal rgb(38,38,48) matching the panda's dark patches with a thick
uniform dark outline; the capital E is filled in the signature badge-red
rgb(220,60,50) to echo the mascot's chest E-badge, with a soft warm-gold
rgb(255,190,30) accent glow. The tiny tagline is warm muted brown-pink
rgb(160,120,100). Scatter a few small cute hand-drawn pixel-art doodles in
the empty cream space AROUND the text — never on or overlapping the
letters — such as a four-point sparkle, a little star, a tiny heart, a
bamboo leaf, a small swirl and a couple of dots, lightly coloured in the
brand accents (coral-pink rgb(255,93,104), gold rgb(255,190,30), teal
rgb(0,180,165), green rgb(60,200,100)), kept sparse and balanced so the
wordmark stays the focus. Crisp legible typography, cute kawaii pixel-art
brand feel, no mascot, no panda, no other words. Flat even lighting, warm
cream white background rgb(255,248,235), wide horizontal 16:9 crop, no
watermark.
